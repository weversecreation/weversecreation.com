Woven from Tradition, Crafted for the World

Our story begins not with a factory, but with a thread. Deep in Karur, the heart of India's textile hub, the spirit of the raatai—the traditional spinning wheel—guides our craft. It’s a symbol of our heritage, a reminder that every great textile begins with a commitment to the purity of its source. This philosophy, which we call 'Living with Nature,' is woven into everything we do.

We honor our roots by exclusively using natural fibers like cotton, linen, and silk, transforming them through innovative weaving techniques that bring their unique textures to life. Each piece is consciously crafted, blending timeless tradition with modern artistry to create textiles that are both beautiful and responsible.

This dedication has been the cornerstone of our global presence. For over 25 years, we have been a trusted supplier to the ScanTex family, a legacy of partnership built on unwavering quality and reliability. It is our promise to deliver superior textiles with a deep sense of responsibility—to our partners, our people, and our planet.
