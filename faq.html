Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common inquiries about our products and processes.

We manufacture a comprehensive range of home textiles. This includes a full collection of Table & Kitchen Linen, such as elegant tablecloths, runners, stylish aprons, and towels. We also produce Living & Bed Linen, like premium bed sheets, duvet covers, cushions, and throws. Additionally, we offer a diverse array of other made-up products, including bath robes, shopping bags, sun caps, and festive Christmas decorations.

All of our products are manufactured in Karur, India, which is known as the country's textile hub for its superior craftsmanship. Our entire production process is handled in-house at our 5,000 sq ft facility, which ensures end-to-end quality control and faster turnaround times.

Yes, we are a global manufacturer and exporter. We have extensive experience supplying international retail markets and are proud to have been a trusted supplier to the ScanTex family in Europe for over 25 years. We also actively participate in leading international trade fairs like Heimtextil in Frankfurt and the Hong Kong Mega Show to connect with buyers from around the world.

We primarily use natural fibers like cotton, linen, and silk, and can assist you in selecting the perfect materials and designs for your brand.

Our commitment to sustainability is central to our "Living With Nature" philosophy. We prioritize the use of natural fibers and eco-conscious designs. Furthermore, we hold key certifications like OEKO-TEX, which guarantees that our textiles are tested for and free from harmful substances, making them safe for people and kinder to the planet.
